Me

I'm Sam Liman, a Software Developer based in Denver, Colorado.

In addition to all things tech, I am drawn towards a myriad of creative endeavors, including photography, cooking, programming, and therapeutic massage, to name a few.

I began working with computers in 2015 as an 'IT Guy', moving back to my hometown, Steamboat Springs, Colorado, to pursue this opportunity. After 4 years in the field, I felt stuck. Software development felt like a natural evolution on this path.

The skills I shaped while working in IT have proven invaluable to me as a software developer, and I am thrilled to be able to to exercise them within my creativity as a software developer.
